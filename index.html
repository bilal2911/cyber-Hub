Required Documents Guide

Don't make multiple trips! Check the exact documents you need to bring to our shop for processing or loan submissions.

To change or correct spellings/surnames, bring the following:

  • ✓ Old Aadhaar Card (Copy and original)
  • ✓ Identity Proof (Voter ID, Passport, PAN Card, or Driving License)
  • ✓ Address Proof (Electricity Bill, Water Bill, Rent Agreement)

To correct your date of birth, bring either of these:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (Original)
  • ✓ Birth Certificate (Issued by Municipal Corp) OR 10th Class Marksheet / Passing Certificate

For photo/biometric updates, very minimal documentation is required:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (Original only; photo will be captured live at center)

To apply for a fresh PAN Card, please bring:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (Linked with working mobile number for e-sign OTP)
  • ✓ Passport Size Photo (Two recent clean prints)
  • ✓ Signature (Will be done on application paper in shop)

For correcting spelling or updating data on your existing PAN Card:

  • ✓ Old PAN Card (Original card or copy)
  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (With updated matching information)

Documents checklist for obtaining home mortgage financing:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card & PAN Card (Applicant & Co-applicant)
  • ✓ Salary Slip (Last 3 to 6 months for salaried individuals)
  • ✓ Bank Statement (Last 6 to 12 months, active account)
  • ✓ Property Documents (Registry copy, Chain documents, Approved map)

Requirements to purchase or finance a brand new or pre-owned car or motorcycle:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card & PAN Card
  • ✓ Bank Statement (Last 6 months showing active salaries/income)
  • ✓ Income Proof (Form 16 / ITR copy or recent Salary Slips)

To grow your business enterprise with secure financial backing, bring:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card & PAN Card (Partners / Proprietor)
  • ✓ GST Registration / Business Incorporation Proof (Shop License, MSME cert)
  • ✓ Bank Statement (Last 12 months for the business current account)

To unlock liquidity using your existing built-up properties/land plots:

  • ✓ Property Papers (Original sale deed, title chain, tax receipt)
  • ✓ Aadhaar Card & PAN Card
  • ✓ Income Proof / ITR (Last 2-3 years income tax filings)
